BACK TO THE ISLAND

Back then in 2006, I went to explore the very far and beautiful New Zealand. Once I landed at Christchurch Airport, I felt like I never felt before. Floods of emotional excitements which result with happiness, very high energy, a bit high mentally, such an amazing feelings which kept during the whole trip. My vanny took me all around the South and North Islands. I was loaded with heaps of cherished memories. That's my photography Journey.
